About Me

My name is Dylan Griess, and I am just beginning my journey as a software developer. I completed my undergraduate work from the University of Iowa in 2016, and went on to complete graduate work from Carroll University in 2019. I worked as an Occupational Therapist for 3 years in an inpatient acute hospital, an assisted living facility, and a dementia care unit. I decided to make a large career transition from the healthcare industry to Denver University's coding bootcamp to pursue a new passion. Other interests of mine in Colorado include roadtripping, camping, biking, and hockey. I also enjoy exploring Denver's social scene, and continuously getting more familiar with my city.

I am always open to meet and collaborate with others on projects. Feel free to reach out to connect on any platforms!
